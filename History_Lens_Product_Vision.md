# History Lens
## Point Your Camera at Anything. Understand Why It Exists.

### Vision
History Lens is an AI-powered mobile app that transforms the camera into a "history lens." Users point their phone at any object, sign, building, landmark, plant, product, tool, or location and instantly receive:

- What it is
- Why it exists
- When it originated
- How it evolved over time
- Historical context
- Related stories
- Videos and visual explainers
- Interactive timelines
- Kid-friendly explanations

The goal is to answer the question:

> "Why is that there?"

especially for naturally curious people and children.

---

# Problem

Children and adults constantly encounter objects and environments they don't understand.

Examples:

- Stop signs
- Fire hydrants
- Street lights
- Bridges
- Historic buildings
- Trains
- Statues
- Trees
- Everyday household objects

Current solutions require:

1. Taking a photo
2. Identifying the object
3. Searching manually
4. Reading fragmented information

History Lens compresses this into a single interaction.

# Example User Journey

### Stop Sign

User points camera at a stop sign.

The app identifies:

- Stop sign
- Location
- Nearby intersection

The app returns:

### What Is It?

A regulatory traffic sign that requires vehicles to come to a complete stop.

### Why Was It Invented?

Created in 1915 as automobiles became more common.

### Evolution

- 1915: First stop signs
- 1920s: Yellow signs
- 1950s: Standardized octagon shape
- 1960s: Red reflective signs

### Local Context

- Installed at this intersection in 1988
- Nearby traffic improvements in 2002

### Media

- 30-second explainer video
- Historical photos
- Timeline animation

### Kid Mode

"Before cars became common, roads were chaotic. Stop signs were invented to help everyone take turns safely."
